────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

6) CHAPTER/PART LABEL GUARANTEE
Every output PART must begin with exactly two header lines:

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Every PART must include at least 3 of these 5 elements:
1) Dialogue exchange (pressure, bargaining, threat, doctrine)
2) A concrete logistics detail (ammo/filter/fuel/transport/tribute)
3) A physical Nurgle sensory detail (rot/flies/spores/rust/cough/seep)
4) A consequence that lands (death, punishment, loss, fracture)
5) A “documentary artifact” as a story object (vox playback, pict-feed, order sheet, casualty sheet)
